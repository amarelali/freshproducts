FRESH PRODUCTS were founded by Ahmad Mostafa Khodr in 2011 in Lebanon. Ahmad Mostafa Khodr had started to work in the agricultural business in 1996. He had been drawn to the idea of challenging himself to enhance the agricultural sector in Lebanon starting by planting phase till the distribution phase and these processes happen by following local and European standards and conditions.
